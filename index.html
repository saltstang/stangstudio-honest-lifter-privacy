Privacy Policy — Honest Lifter

Effective 2026-07-15 · Stangstudio

The short version

Honest Lifter collects no data. None. There is no account, no sign-in, no analytics, no ads, no tracking, and no server. Everything you log stays in a database on your own device.

What the app stores, and where

Your workouts, exercises, plans, and settings are saved in a local database on your phone. This data never leaves your device unless you yourself export or share it. Uninstalling the app deletes it.

Sharing and exporting

The app can create export files (JSON, CSV) and share codes on your request. These are handed to the app you choose (email, messenger, file manager) via your phone's standard share dialog. We never see them — we have nothing to see them with.

Purchases

Honest Lifter is bought once through Google Play. The purchase is handled entirely by Google; we do not receive or store any payment or identity information. See Google's privacy policy for how Google processes purchases.

Permissions

The app works offline and requests no sensitive permissions — no location, no contacts, no camera, no microphone.

Children

The app is a general-audience workout logger. Since it collects no data from anyone, it collects no data from children either.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be published at this address with a new effective date. Given the app's design — no data collection, by principle — meaningful changes are not expected.
